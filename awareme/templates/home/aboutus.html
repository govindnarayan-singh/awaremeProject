what we are trying to solve?

We are trying to reduce the gap between government organizations/ NGOs and citizens by providing them one single platform for collecting information related to any social awareness program which is applicable for them based on their search. By the end of our project user would be able to make online registrations even they would be able to donate online to any organizations that they prefer. Our project will help citizens to stay update related to any schemes and government facilities.

our motive...

Our main motive behind this is to help people get aware about social campaigns. When we look around we see that, there is a need of making this particular kind of platform which will help people to interact with various NGO’S ,Social camps and government schemes.

problem that we want to solve

The problem is people are unaware about the social camps due to lack of information and also because of hectic lifestyle. We can see that people have lost their interest in any social activities due to which society is affected and humanity is lost.Even if social camps are held very few people take part in it.

main purpose

Our main objective is to create a platform through which people will become socially more active as they will get to know about the particular camps. This will help people to get aware about what is going on in our society.

making use of online Payment Gateway

With the exponential growth of donations on the online platforms in recent years, promoting a culture of donation and social services within the community through the web platform available on smart phones is the next step

The AWARE ME makes it easy for people and residents to donate with a single click from anywhere and at any time.This web application helps you to get notified about nearby social welfare programs such as Donation camps or anyother NGO's to get in touch with them for social cause

what we support..
